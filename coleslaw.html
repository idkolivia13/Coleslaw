Coleslaw

Ingredients

    • 1 white cabbage
    • 4 carrots;peeled
    • 1 large red onion
    • Parsley or coriander;finely chopped
    • 1 tbsp mustard
    • 50g mayonnaise
    • 50g yogurt or more mayo
    • 1-2 tbsp white wine vinegar
    • A few pinches of paprika

Instructions

1.Slice the cabbage as finely as you can into thin shreds.Tip into a bowl.

2.Grate the carrots on a box grater to coarsely shred, or cut into thin strips.Tip into the bowl. Finely slice the onion, and thin as you can, and add to the bowl with the other veg. Add the herbs if using.

3.Whisk the mustard, mayo, yogurt and vinegar. Season well, and taste for sharpness and creamy. Add more vinegar if you like.

4.Tip the dressing into the veg bowl, and mix everything together well with a large spoon. Stir so all the veg gets coated lightly in the dressing. Sprinkle with a few pinches of paprika, and serve straight away. Can be covered and chilled for up to 3 days. Mix well before serving

5.You can serve your classic homemade coleslaw as a salad or with any meal of your choice.
